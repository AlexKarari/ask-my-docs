# Support & Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I contact support?
- Email: support@HealthierYouclinic.com
- Response time: **within 48 business hours**
- Phone support is **appointment-only**

---

## Can I pause my program?
Yes — once per program.

> **Unique Rule:**  
> A pause may last **no longer than 30 consecutive days**.

---

## What happens if I miss appointments?
- First miss: reminder + reschedule
- Second miss: clinician review
- Third miss: program suspension

---

## Do you guarantee weight loss?
No.

> **Signature Statement:**  
> “We guarantee medical care — not a number on the scale.”
