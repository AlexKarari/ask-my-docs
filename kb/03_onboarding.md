# Patient Onboarding Process

Onboarding at HealthierYou is **structured, sequential, and mandatory**.

## Step-by-Step Onboarding

### Step 1: Intake & Eligibility Review
- Online intake form
- Medical history review
- BMI and comorbidity screening

> Patients missing **two or more required fields** are automatically deferred.

---

### Step 2: Initial Medical Consultation
- 60-minute virtual or in-person visit
- Conducted by a licensed bariatric physician
- Determines program eligibility

> **Unique Detail:**  
> No treatment plan is issued during the first consultation.

---

### Step 3: Metabolic Baseline Report (MBR™)
- Lab work + clinical analysis
- Delivered within **14 calendar days**
- Forms the foundation of all treatment plans

---

### Step 4: Program Assignment
- Assigned only after MBR™ review
- Requires **dual clinician approval**
- Patients may accept or decline without penalty
