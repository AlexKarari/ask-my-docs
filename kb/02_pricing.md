# Pricing & Program Structure

HealthierYou Bariatric Clinic operates on a **transparent, tiered pricing model**.
There are **no hidden fees** and **no bundled procedures** without consent.

## Core Programs

### 1. Foundation Medical Weight Program
**Cost:** $2,400 total  
**Duration:** 6 months  

Includes:
- Initial physician evaluation
- Monthly clinician check-ins
- Lab interpretation & adjustments
- Nutrition coaching (non-meal-based)

> **Unique Rule:**  
> The Foundation Program is the **only program** that does NOT include medication.

---

### 2. Advanced Metabolic Reset Program
**Cost:** $4,800 total  
**Duration:** 9 months  

Includes:
- Everything in Foundation, plus:
- FDA-approved weight-loss medications
- Quarterly metabolic re-testing
- Behavioral therapy sessions

> **Unique Identifier:**  
> This is the **only program** that includes *quarterly metabolic re-testing*.
