# Data Privacy & Medical Information Handling

HealthierYou Bariatric Clinic treats patient data as **clinical-grade medical records**, not consumer wellness data.

## Data We Collect
- Medical history
- Laboratory results
- Appointment notes
- Program adherence metrics

## Data Storage & Retention
- All records are stored in **HIPAA-compliant systems**
- Data is retained for **exactly 7 years**, then permanently deleted
- Backups are encrypted using **AES-256**
